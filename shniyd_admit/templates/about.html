The Sheikh Hasina National Institute of Youth Development is a premier youth development organization in Bangladesh, dedicated to empowering the youth and helping them achieve their full potential. With a focus on leadership development, skills training, and community engagement, the Institute has been making a positive impact on the lives of young people for over two decades. Our mission is to create a world where young people are equipped and motivated to lead, serve, and make a difference in their communities.

Sheikh Hasina National Institute of Youth Development (SHNIYD) is a premier institution dedicated to the development and empowerment of youth in Bangladesh. Established in the year 2017, the institute has been at the forefront of creating opportunities and providing support for young individuals to reach their full potential.

SHNIYD offers a wide range of programs and services aimed at promoting youth development and leadership, including training and capacity building, career counseling, and mentorship opportunities. With a focus on innovation and sustainability, the institute is committed to creating a positive impact in the lives of young people and the communities they serve.
